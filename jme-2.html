JME also known as Jamie Adenuga is a British grime MC, songwriter, record producer and DJ. As of 2023, JME’s net worth is $6 million. He acclaimed fame as a co-founder of a full-fledged record label titled “Boy Better Know” with his brother in 2008.

He has been active in his profession since 2006 and has earned enormous fame and fortune. He has accumulated a massive net worth of about $3 million.

In May 2015, he launched a studio album titled “Integrity>”, which secured 12th position on “UK Albums Chart” and also got a nomination for the “IMPALA Album of the Year Award”.

His most successful single titled “96 Fuckries” ranked the third number on the “UK Indie Chart”. He has collaborated with many famous music artists such as “Shorty”, “Frisco”, “Capo Lee” and others.

Career

JME started his career in 2006 by creating mobile phone ringtones.

He released his debut album titled “Famous?” consisting of 15 songs in 2008.

In October 2010, he released another album titled “Blam!”, featuring the singles “Over Me”, “Sidetracked”, and “CD is Dead”.

He released a compilation album titled “History:” in February 2011, which secured 162 numbers on the “UK Albums Chart”.

He released his third studio album titled “Integrity>” in May 2015, ranked twelfth position on the “UK Albums Chart”. After about four years on 29 November 2019, he released his fourth studio album titled “Grime MC” getting 8/10 by music and fashion magazine “Clash” and was also highly appreciated by American online music publication “Pitchfork”.

Afterward, in 2020-2021, he collaborated with several famous rappers and musicians such as “Skepta”, “Jammer”, “Shorty”, “Frisco” and others.

In March 2021, he released a collaborative album titled “Norf Face” along with “Capo Lee”, “Frisco” and “Shorty”.
