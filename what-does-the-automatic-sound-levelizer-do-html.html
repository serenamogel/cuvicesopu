What does the automatic sound Levelizer do?

February 29, 2024
blog

Answer

ASL stands for AUTOMATIC SOUND LEVELIZER (Type 1 and Type 2 only)

When the audio sound becomes difficult to hear while driving owing to road noise, wind noise, and other factors, the system automatically changes the volume and tone quality to the most appropriate levels for the current noise level.

What exactly is ASL on the Lexus radio?

When ASL is activated, the system automatically changes the loudness and tone quality in response to vehicle speed—in other words, it takes into consideration the ambient noise of a moving vehicle and makes modifications on a continuous basis to maintain the sound sharp.

What exactly is ASL in a Prius may be found here.

ASL is an abbreviation for Auto Sound Levelling, and it works by adjusting the volume up or down in an effort to combat ambient noise that is present in the vehicle. For example, the quicker you travel, the louder the road noise becomes, causing the radio level to increase in order to compensate.

What is the definition of treble in audio?

Treble refers to tones that have a frequency or range that is at the upper end of the human hearing range. In music, this relates to the concept of “high notes.” When notating such notes, the treble clef is often used. The treble sound is the polar opposite of the bass sound.
