Today is Bonnie Bella’s fourth birthday, and her parents are ready to celebrate. Joseline Hernandez gave her daughter a holiday shoot ahead of Bonnie’s special day.

Bonnie Bella was the princess of the photoshoot with a white dress and matching tiara.

“Today, my family and I are healthy, happy, and together,” Joseline told her social media fans. “We cherish each second,” the celebrity mom added. “Love is in the air,” Hernandez declared.

Joseline and her daughter’s father, Stevie J, have managed to maintain a sense of normalcy despite the irregularities of this year. Bonnie Bella was quarantined with her father in California earlier this year because of the COVID-19 shutdown, which restricted flights and other forms of public transportation. Bonnie ultimately reunited with her mom and has been able to enjoy quality time at both her mom and dad’s homes ever since.

Joseline Hernandez and Stevie J got off to a bit of a rocky start in terms of co-parenting. The celebrities, however, managed to mend things to cultivate healthy relations.

“Whether you go through arguments, fights, court system … anything that you go through, it’s going to get better,” Joseline said during a recent interview with Hot 97. “It’s going to get ugly before it gets better, but it’ll always get better,” the celebrity mom added.
