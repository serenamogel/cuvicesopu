Busy Philipps 'heartbroken' over the loss of her 'beautiful' BFF of 30 years, Kate Cooper Serge: 'I'

February 11, 2024
blog

Girls5Eva star Busy Philipps is famously best friends with five-time Oscar nominee Michelle Williams, but she just lost an even older, dearer friend - Kate Cooper Serge - to an undisclosed five-month illness at age 44.

On Thursday, the Illinois-born 44-year-old Instastoried a teary selfie and posted a long heartfelt message about the death of the 'beautiful' New Jersey-based educator, who's 'no longer in pain' after enduring 'the most unfair battle any of us could imagine.'

Busy (born Elizabeth) remarked about how Kate was the 'best mom' to two 'beautiful' sons from her marriage to 'one of the truly great ones' Larry Serge.

'I have so many things to say about your beautiful, shining, gigantic, megawatt soul (like your perfect smile) that radiated so brightly from within you that everyone who came in contact with immediately felt and fell in love with,' Philipps - who boasts 2.8M social media followers - wrote on Thursday.

'But this is Instagram so I won't say them all. But truly, thank god I was able to say them all to you in these past five months. Your grace, your wisdom, your taste, your talent, your creativity, your insane sense of humor, your laugh, your laugh, your laugh. The ease with which you would so clearly see things in a new way. Your absolute fearlessness.'

'Good lord. I had to apologize [for] my ridiculousness. And then we were laughing because your laugh. My sweet girl, you were more than just a sparkly person - you shined like the goddamn sun. It's the honor of my existence that you called me your best friend for the past 30 years. 

'It wasn't nearly enough time on earth, and honey, I'm so so sorry you had to leave the party early but I promise you that I will do all the things. And I know you'll do all of the things where you are now bathed in that light.'

Philipps vowed to start watching Max's And Just Like that so their mutual friend Christine 'has someone to call and scream with about it.'

'Earlier this week, I said: "I feel like the boy who cried wolf except I'm just the girl who cried about everything. But now it turns out, I didn't know the half of heartbreak like this,"' the Single Drunk Female actress concluded.

'I love you, my girl. Thank you for picking me all those years ago. I'm so blessed to have had your love in my life.'
