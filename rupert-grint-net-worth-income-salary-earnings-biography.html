Rupert Alexander Lloyd Grint, better known by his stage name Rupert Grint, is a well-known English actor. Rupert Grint is probably best known to the general public for his depiction of the fictitious character Ron Weasly in the “Harry Potter” films, who completes the famous triad of characters in the film series. Based on the books written by J. K. Rowling, the fantasy film series “Harry Potter” has become an enormous international hit, with eight films in the series having been produced to far, which have together earned more than $7.7 billion at box offices across the globe. The first film in the series, “Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone,” was released in 2001 to widespread critical acclaim and a worldwide revenue of more than $980 million at the box office. “Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets,” which proved to be a huge hit, was released in 2002, starring Rupert Grint, Daniel Radcliffe, and Emma Watson in the lead roles.  “Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Part 2” was the title of the last chapter in the series, which was released in 2011 and earned an even greater amount of money at the box office than the previous instalment, grossing more than $1.3 billion worldwide. The “Harry Potter” films, which are widely regarded as the highest-grossing film series of all time, have spawned a slew of products and a spin-off series called “Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them,” which is set to premiere in 2016 and is based on the book series of the same name. Without a doubt, the popularity of the series had a significant effect on the total renown of its main performers, all of whom have gone on to achieve considerable success in their respective fields.
