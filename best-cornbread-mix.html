Best Cornbread Mix to Buy at the Grocery Store

April 27, 2024
blog

The best cornbread mix makes cornbread that is fluffy, thick, and a little bit sweet. It should remind you of biting into an ear of buttered corn on a summer day. It should be perfect when eaten alone with butter or sensational at sopping up chili.

Luckily, there are tons of options for really good cornbread mix that makes it easy to bake up a pan when the craving strikes you. Different styles and flavors of cornbread mix line the shelves at most grocery stores. Because of that, it can be hard to decide which you should buy: homestyle, honey butter, gluten-free? It can feel overwhelming. Thankfully, you don’t need to worry. I spent two full days making every store-bought cornbread box mix I could find. I’m very tired, but here are my findings.

Best Gluten-Free

King Arthur Gluten-Free Cornbread MixAmazon

I literally cannot believe that King Arthur’s Gluten-Free Cornbread Mix is gluten-free. I kept double-checking the box to make sure. This gluten-free cornbread mix stood up to all the gluten-full cornbread mixes we tried. And it was definitely way better than any other gluten-free cornbread mix we tasted. If you are gluten intolerant or sensitive, this is one of the best alternatives I’ve ever had for a bread-based product. Good job, King Arthur! But what did I expect? He is the king after all.

Best Honey Butter

Zatarain’s Honey Butter Cornbread MixWalmart

I know Zatarain’s knows rice, but I’m just finding out they also know cornbread mix! Their honey butter cornbread mix is delicious. It bakes up really tender and light. The honey flavor is pronounced without being overpowering. It’s super buttery. It’s the best cornbread mix to pair with a box of Zatarain’s Jambalaya for a delicious, cheap, faux-Cajun meal. To be honest, this cornbread doesn’t even need butter. It’s that good.

Best Sweet

Famous Dave’s Cornbread MixWalmart

Be warned: This cornbread mix is very, very sweet. It might not appeal to everyone. That being said, it definitely appealed to me. It’s so soft and tasty. If you’re from the South, your grandfather would think this was the best cornbread mix. He would love to eat a bowl of this cornbread soaking in buttermilk. If you’re not an old Southern person, you’d probably prefer this topped with hot honey or butter. I think all ways are correct!

Credit: Liv Averett / Walmart

Rating:

9.5/10

Best Spicy

Famous Dave’s Jalapeno Cornbread MixWalmart

If you can handle a little heat, I recommend getting a box of Famous Dave’s Jalapeno Cornbread. It’s my personal favorite. It’s so soft and definitely packs a punch of jalapeno-y goodness. This would be so delicious with chili. Or topped with salted butter and a little bit of honey. Honestly, I don’t think there’s anything you could do with this that wouldn’t be good. I don’t know Famous Dave, but I certainly would like to so I could thank him for making the best cornbread mix.

Credit: Liv Averett / Walmart

Best of the Best

Jiffy Corn Muffin MixTarget

They might as well put a picture of this box of cornbread in the dictionary under the word “cornbread” (even though they call this corn muffin mix). Jiffy is the industry standard for cornbread mix. It bakes up moist and tender. The flavor is sweet, but not too sweet. It’s very mild and standard in the perfect way. That’s what makes it the best cornbread mix. Be warned, though: This is made with lard and is not suitable for vegetarians.
