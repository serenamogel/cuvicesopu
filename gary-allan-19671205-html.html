Gary Allan Herzberg is an American country music artist. Signing with Decca in 1996, Allan made his debut in country music with the release of the single "Her Man", a prelude to his certified gold debut album, Heart for Sale, released in 1996 on Decca. His second album, It Will Be You, followed in 1998. Allan's third album, Smoke Rings in the Dark, was his debut album for MCA Nashville and was his first platinum album. His follow-up albums, Alright Guy and See If I Care, are both certified platinum while Tough All Over and Greatest Hits and Living Hard are both gold certified. His next two albums, Get Off on the Pain and Set You Free, both entered the Top 10 on the US Billboard Top Country Albums chart, at No. 2 and 1, respectively
