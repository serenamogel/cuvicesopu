Houses and Cars

Houses

Pharrell Williams has an impressive real estate portfolio. He has purchased and sold several pieces of real estate across the United States.

He purchased a 9,000 square feet waterfront house in Virginia Beach, Virginia, in 2001 for $1.85 million.

Likewise, Pharrell purchased a Miami Beach penthouse apartment unit for $8.5 million in 2007. Also, he purchased another triplex penthouse in Miami for $12.5 million.

He put the 9,000 square feet unit in the market for $17 million in 2012. With not many interested buyers, Pharrell ultimately accepted $9.35 million for the unit in 2015.

Similarly, Pharrell purchased a glass mansion located between the mountains of Beverly Hills and Sherman Oaks, California, in 2018 from film and television tycoon Tyler Perry for $15.6 million.

However, the ultra-contemporary mansion is back in the market for $17 million.

Moreover, the rapper purchased a modern 5,800 square feet mansion back in 2015 for $7.1 million. Just like his glass mansion Pharrell listed this property in 2020 for $11.95 million.

The Los Angeles homes are far from the only properties Pharrell owns.

In April of 2020, during the outbreak of the COVID-19 pandemic, Pharrell paid about $30 million for a 17,000 square feet waterfront abode in Coral Gables, Florida.

The property that sits on 3.5 acres of land was previously listed for $45 million.

Charity

Williams is very generous when it comes to helping the needy. He built an afterschool center in Virginia Beach, his hometown, with a total cost of $35 million.

Similarly, he offered internships to more than 114 Harlem High School students.

All the students were accepted to college, and the purpose of the rapper’s donation was to help the young students become the next leaders who will enact real change.

Likewise, Pharrell owns a non-profit organization, From One Hand to Another (FOHTA).

The main objective of the organization is to modernize the traditional community center to empower underserved children by giving access to media, art, and new technology.

Moreover, Pharrell teamed up with Verizon to launch a music curriculum in a national campaign for Verizon Foundation Learning school in April of 2019.

The foundation endeavors to provide internet access and free technology to under-resourced middle schools.

Career

Pharrell Williams embarked on a solo music career in 2003. His debut studio album ‘In My Mind‘ hit the charts in 2006.

Besides releasing the chart-topping albums and singles, Pharell also continued producing tracks for artists such as Britney Spears, Beyoncé, and Madonna.

He produced Madonna’s eleventh album, Hard Candy, in 2008.

Similarly, he also produced and co-wrote the song Fresh Out of the Oven for none other than Jennifer Lopez.

He released his second studio album, Girl, in 2014, which reached No. 1 on the R&B chart in the United States, Denmark, Switzerland, UK, and Australia.

One of his most iconic singles, Happy, was released in 2013, reached No.1 in 10 countries, and was certified seven times platinum by RIAA.
