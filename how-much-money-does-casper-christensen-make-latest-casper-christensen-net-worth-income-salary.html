Casper Lindholm Christensen (born 22 August 1968 in Birkerd, Denmark) is a Danish comedian who has been on many television shows. He was born in Copenhagen, Denmark. He also appeared in the Danish sitcoms Langt fra Las Vegas (Far Away from Las Vegas) and Klovn (Clown/Fool), in which he had a major part.

He began his television career in 1991 with the Danish children’s programme Hvaffor en Hnd?
