Ever wonder what Beyonce would look like as a bride on her wedding day? Well wonder no more, a new music video for her song Best Thing I Never Had was released today and it shows us just that. Beyonce as a bride on her wedding day, and boy is she beautiful.

Beyonce is at her best here, both in appearance and vocals. The classy and stylish video features the stunning singer on the most important day of her life. We watch her as she tells her ex-boyfriend that it “sucks to be you right now,” as she gets back at him by marrying another man.

With the music video for the diva’s latest single from her new album 4, we get a memorable and wonderfully done visual backed by Beyonce‘s passionate lyrics.
