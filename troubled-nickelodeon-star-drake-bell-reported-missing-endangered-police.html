Troubled Nickelodeon star Drake Bell reported missing, endangered: police

April 27, 2024
blog

Explore More

Drake Bell is missing, TMZ has reported.

“He is considered missing and endangered,” wrote Florida law enforcement in a Facebook notice posted Thursday.

According to the Daytona Beach Police Department, the actor born Jared Drake Bell was last seen driving a 2022 gray BMW on Wednesday just before 9 p.m., potentially around the area of Mainland HS.

The 36-year-old “Drake & Josh” star has been at the center of several allegations of abuse, including accusations from a woman who had been in a relationship with Bell.

Bell also recently finished probation for an attempted child endangerment case with a 15-year-old girl, to which he pleaded guilty.

He also filed for bankruptcy in 2014, and was arrested for driving under the influence in 2015.

Anyone with information about Bell’s current whereabouts is urged to contact Daytona Beach police at (386) 671-5207.
