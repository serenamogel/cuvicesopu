Maurice Richard Povich, better known by his stage name Maury Povich, is an American journalist, talk show presenter, actor, film producer, and television anchor. Maury Povich is best known to the general public as the host of the popular talk programme “Maury.” The programme first broadcast on television in 1991 and has now shown for 22 seasons. “Maury” typically covers a variety of problems, such as domestic abuse, adolescent pregnancy, bullying, different rare diseases, and many more, with an emphasis on topical topics. Over time, the name “Maury” became synonymous with paternity tests and polygraph examinations. Despite being often chastised for exploiting serious problems for the purpose of amusement, “Maury” has maintained high enough ratings to broadcast 2,640 episodes to far.
