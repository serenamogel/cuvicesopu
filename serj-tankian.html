Serj Tankian turned 56 years old on August 21. To mark the occasion, Tom Morello shared a collection of old pictures of himself and his “beloved brother” on Instagram. “Happy birthday to my beloved brother Mr. @serjtankian!” the Rage Against the Machine guitarist said in the description of the photo, which featured pictures of the duo over the years.

The most recent project that Morello and the System of a Down singer worked on together was a cover of Gang of Four’s “Natural’s Not In It” for an album dedicated to the band’s late co-founder Andy Gill.

For the first time in 15 years, SOAD released brand-new music in 2020 with “Protect the Land” and “Genocidal Humanoidz,” but they haven’t done so since. The band “will be making an announcement about something” in 2023, according to Tankian, but that date is quickly approaching. Aside from the songs, SOAD hasn’t put anything else out since 2006. John Dolmayan complained about Tankian earlier this year, claiming that they “should have moved on” without him rather than taking a hiatus almost 20 years ago.
